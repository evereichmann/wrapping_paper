About


Who wants to buy an entire roll of paper for a small present.
You can make a custom wrapping paper in this application.
Super easy, super fun, and super free! Just follow the step to
the right and you are good to go!

Thanks for using Wrap It!



Instructions

  1. Select a layout and hit the submit button
  2. Layout One
    • Go to the web, find an image, and get the image url
    • Write a short text input (we are working on a text wrapping funcation at the moment)
  3. Layout Two
    • Go to the web, find two images, and get the images url
  4. Press submit!
  5. If you want to change the wrapping paper hit clear make your changes and submit again
  6. Copy and Paste the image into any text document and print
